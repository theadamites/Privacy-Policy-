Privacy Policy for Rutgers LSAMP Alumni API

Effective Date: [11/11/2023]

Introduction:

This Privacy Policy governs the collection, usage, and protection of public information through the LinkedIn Alumni API for the LSAMP program at Rutgers. We are committed to safeguarding the privacy and security of data and ensuring compliance with applicable data protection laws, LinkedIn's policies, and the LinkedIn API Terms of Use.

Data Collection:

We collect publicly available data from LinkedIn profiles of individuals who are alumni of the LSAMP program at Rutgers University. This data includes, but is not limited to, job history, location, salary information (if available), and higher education qualifications.

Purpose of Data Collection:

The primary purpose of collecting this data is to conduct research and analysis focused on assessing the positive impact of the LSAMP program on its alumni. This analysis is pivotal in our efforts to secure funding for program enhancements, improvements, and continuation. The data will be exclusively utilized for this research project.

Data Anonymization:

To ensure utmost privacy, all collected data is anonymized and stripped of personally identifiable information (PII). This includes excluding names, contact details, and specific LinkedIn profile URLs. The data is aggregated and anonymized to protect the privacy of individuals.

Data Security:

We employ stringent security measures to safeguard the data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Access to the collected data is restricted solely to authorized personnel directly engaged in the research project.

Data Usage:

The data collected will be exclusively utilized for research purposes related to the LSAMP program. It will not be employed for marketing, commercial endeavors, or shared with third parties. Our commitment is to maintain the integrity and confidentiality of the data.

Data Retention:

All collected data will be retained only for the duration of the research project. Upon the conclusion of the study, the data will be securely and irrevocably deleted to ensure compliance with privacy standards, LinkedIn's policies, and the LinkedIn API Terms of Use.

Data Usage Consent:

By accessing the Rutgers LSAMP Alumni API, you acknowledge and agree that the data collected from LinkedIn profiles of alumni will be used exclusively for research purposes related to the LSAMP program at Rutgers. You further consent to comply with LinkedIn's terms and conditions, including not using the collected data for any unauthorized purposes, marketing, or other activities prohibited by LinkedIn's policies.

Legal Compliance:

We diligently adhere to all applicable laws and regulations governing data privacy and protection, including but not limited to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), as well as LinkedIn's API Terms of Use and policies.
